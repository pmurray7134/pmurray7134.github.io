Peculiar Muskox Inc.

Have you ever heard of truffle-sniffing pigs? Well we use very strange muskoxen in a similar fashion to find magic beans! We were founded by the wizard who gave Jack the beans that grew into a magic beanstalk, and we are now selling to the public!

Our founding wizard used a peculiar breed of muskoxen, who can sniff up these magic beans buried in the ground! These special beans, found by our peculiar muskoxen, will take you up to the tippy-top of the world! Meet and greet giants, and learn about their walk of life!
